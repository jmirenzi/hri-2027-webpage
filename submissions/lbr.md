Late Breaking Reports (LBRs) provide an opportunity to share recent, in-progress, or preliminary work with the HRI community. LBRs are ideal for novel ideas, work-in-progress, and results that are not yet mature enough for a full paper submission.

## Format

LBR submissions must be **2 pages** (excluding references) in the ACM sigconf double-column format.

Accepted LBRs will be included in the ACM Digital Library and presented as posters at the conference.

## Topics

LBRs are welcome on any topic relevant to HRI, including but not limited to preliminary results, replication studies, negative results, and novel research directions.

## Anonymization

LBR submissions should follow the same double-blind anonymization guidelines as full papers. Remove all author-identifying information from the manuscript.

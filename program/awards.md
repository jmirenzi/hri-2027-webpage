HRI 2027 will recognize outstanding contributions to the field through several awards. Recipients will be announced at the conference.

## Best Paper Award

Awarded to the full paper judged to make the most significant contribution to the field of human-robot interaction.

## Best Paper Honorable Mention

Awarded to full papers that are runners-up for the Best Paper Award.

## Best Video Award

Awarded to the submitted video that best captures and communicates HRI research to a broad audience.

## Best Late Breaking Report Award

Awarded to the LBR judged most promising or impactful.

## Best Workshop Paper Award

Awarded to the best paper submitted to a co-located workshop.

---

Award recipients from previous years can be found at [humanrobotinteraction.org](https://humanrobotinteraction.org).

HRI 2027 will host an Industry Day providing a dedicated forum for researchers from industry to present their work and engage with the broader HRI community.

## About Industry Day

Industry Day is an opportunity to:
- Showcase applied HRI research and deployed systems
- Foster collaboration between academic and industry researchers
- Discuss real-world challenges and open problems in HRI
